Symbian SDL

Based on SDL 1.2.14

Installing:
Download that from libsdl.org and extract on your developement directory.
Extract sdl-1.2.14-s60-x.x.x.zip on your developement directory.
Run install from SDL_Install

Binaries are in SDL-1.2.14/symbian/install folder to copy into your phone.
